## KEY INSIGHTS SYNTHESIS

### The Low Ticket Paradox
- Margin is low → Volume is king
- Solution: Visibility strategy (Mercado Líder badge or Premium ads)
- Not about price, about appearance

### The Seasonality Advantage
- 3-week prediction = competitive edge
- 15% cost reduction possible
- Requires data collection + forecasting

### The Demand Gap Framework
- High search + low supply = opportunity
- But product must exist OR fill experience gap
- Every abandoned cart = intelligence

### The AI Copilot Model
- Machine processes data + identifies patterns
- Humans interpret + decide
- Combination wins

### The Experience Revolution
- Discounts alone no longer work (81% demand "really worthwhile")
- 86% compare extensively
- Free freight, PIX, engagement (lives) matter

### The Micro-Niching Pattern
- <2% Platinum in emerging categories
- Less competition, better margins
- Requires data to identify
